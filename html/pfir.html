PFIR (People For Internet Responsibility)

 

About pfir
English (USA)

This is the mailing list for distribution of PFIR - People For Internet Responsibility announcements, policy statements, and other public communications. More information and archives are available via the PFIR Homepage. Members may choose between immediate delivery of new messages (the default setting), or digests of messages. Please see below to subscribe, unsubscribe, or to change your subscription settings. Subscriptions are free, of course.

Using pfir To post a message to all the list members, send email to pfir@pfir.org.

You can subscribe to the list, or change your existing subscription, in the sections below.
